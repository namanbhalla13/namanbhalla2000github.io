THE MOST AWESOME BOOK OF THE YEAR

THE MOST AWESOME BOOK OF THE YEAR This book by Chitra Banerjee Divakaruni was supposed to be in tandem with her earlier book 'The Palace of Illusions', but I daresay this book on Ramayan from Sita's perspective has come out to be more spectacular, and almost a masterpiece!
Sitayaan. Looking at all episodes of the lives of Ram and Sita from the viewpoint of Sita, exploring the ordeals that a woman has to bear due to society's strictures, how unfairly one can be treated despite being nothing but true and still emerging as the hero, brought tears to my eyes.
Read this if you are a man and you will get to really understand the way women think.
Read this if you are a woman and you will get to not only understand yourself and the society better but also get inspiration on how to speak you mind and hold your head high in the wake of trials and tribulations.
